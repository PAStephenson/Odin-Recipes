Gullinbursti - The Golden Maned Boar

Description

Gullinbursti, meaning "Gold mane" was a boar said to have bristles in its main that glowed in the dark.

The boar was created by the dwarfs Brokkr and Sindri after a bet with Loki.

Ingredients

  • 1 complete pig skin

Steps

  1. Throw the pig skin into a furnace.
  2. Work the furnace bellows
  3. Keep working the furnace bellows, don't stop
  4. Once ready, the boar will emerge from the furnace
