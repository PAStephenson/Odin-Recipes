Gleipnir - Fenrir's Bindings

Description

Gleipnir is the chain that was used by the gods to bind the monstrous wolf Fenrir.

It was crafted by the dwarves and is impossible to break.

The ingredients needed to craft Gleipnir are incredibly rare. Therefore, only the most skilled craftsman should attempt this recipe.

Ingredients

  • The sound of a cat's footsteps
  • The beard of a woman
  • The roots of a mountain
  • The sinews of a bear
  • The breath of a fish
  • The spit of a bird

Steps

  1. Carefully place the ingredients into a furnace
  2. Work the furnace bellows to raise the temperature
  3. Hold the temperature at a constant 5000 °C for 7 days
  4. After 7 days, allow the furnace to cool
  5. Once cool, remove Gleipnir
